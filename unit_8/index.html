About

Kirstin McLatchie

Creative Design + Sustainable Strategy

Born in Scotland but raised in Steinbeck Country, I have a deep love for all things outdoorsy. My love for designed blossomed as I grew up watching Christopher Lowel and other interior designs shows on HGTV. Once I got to college, I found graphic design and knew immediately that what where I wanted to focus my energy.

After graduating from The Art Institute of California—Orange County, I spent a few years being inspired daily at Roadtrip Nation as a graphic designer.

Currently, I work full time for Occidental College as Senior Marketing Manager for Web, Digital & Mobile.
